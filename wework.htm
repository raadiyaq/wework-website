About Me

Hi, I'm Raadiya! I recently graduated from Drexel University with my B.S. in Biomedical Engineering. I have 2 years of combined work experience in analytical and project management roles. I enjoy problem solving and love using Excel whenever possible. I've demonstrated strong leadership and communication skills while managing a team at Bristol-Myers Squibb to develop dashboards of market research data and communicate results to stakeholders. I'm quick on my feet and always excited to learn new skills. I learned SQL while developing a database to monitor inflow and outflow of materials used at several Estee Lauder plants to reduce waste. I'm always looking for ways to improve myself and up for a challenge.
